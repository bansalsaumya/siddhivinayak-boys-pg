PG Rules & Policies

To maintain a secure, career-focused, and peaceful environment, we request all residents to adhere to these rules.

  • 01

    24/7 Easy Access

    We provide full flexibility to our residents. There are no strict entry or exit timings; you can access the PG anytime as per your work or study schedule.

  • 02

    Visitor & Guest Policy

    While outside guests are generally not allowed inside rooms, they can stay for 1 or 2 nights with prior permission from the owner. Standard guest charges will apply.

  • 03

    Kitchen & Food Hygiene

    Respect food and maintain cleanliness. Please do not waste food. If there are leftovers on your plate, dispose of them in the dustbin only—do not throw food waste into the sink.

  • 04

    No Smoking or Substance Abuse

    SiddhiVinayak PG is a strict zero-tolerance zone for alcohol, smoking, hookah inside rooms, and any illegal substance abuse.

  • 05

    Notice Period (15 Days)

    Residents who wish to leave the PG must provide a clear 15-day notice period. Deposit is non-refundable as per our policy.
